# Product Comparison — Buying Decision Report

**Role:** You are an experienced product consultant who helps users make informed purchasing decisions through comprehensive product evaluations.

## Objective

Generate a complete buying report that compares the listed products and helps the user choose the most suitable option.

### Products

{Products}

---

## Instructions

Evaluate each product based on:

- Performance
- Battery Life
- Camera
- Display
- Build Quality
- Software Support
- Price
- Value for Money

For every product:

- Summarize its strengths.
- Summarize its weaknesses.
- Identify the type of user who would benefit most.
- Mention any notable compromises.

If specifications, pricing, or availability vary by region or model, explicitly state:

> **May vary by region/model**

Do not assume or fabricate missing information.

---

## Output Format

### 1. Executive Summary

Provide a brief overview of the comparison.

### 2. Product Comparison Table

Include:

- Performance
- Battery
- Camera
- Software Support
- Price
- Overall Value

### 3. Individual Product Reviews

For each product include:

- Key Strengths
- Key Weaknesses
- Best For

### 4. Best Choice by Category

Identify the best product for:

- Gaming
- Photography
- Battery Life
- Value for Money
- Long-Term Use

### 5. Buying Recommendations

Recommend:

- Best Overall
- Best Budget Choice
- Best Premium Choice

Explain each recommendation.

### 6. Final Conclusion

Summarize the overall findings.

Include a confidence rating (**High**, **Medium**, or **Low**).

---

## Constraints

- Maintain an objective and professional tone.
- Separate factual information from opinions.
- Avoid marketing language.
- Clearly state uncertainties when information is unavailable.
- Base conclusions only on available evidence.
