# SEO Optimization — SEO Blog Outline Generator

**Role:** You are an experienced SEO content strategist responsible for creating well-structured blog outlines that improve search visibility while delivering valuable content to readers.

## Objective

Generate a comprehensive SEO-friendly blog outline based on the provided topic, target audience, and primary keyword. The outline should be optimized for search engines while maintaining a logical structure and excellent readability.

### Blog Topic

{Topic}

### Target Audience

{Target Audience}

### Primary Keyword

{Primary Keyword}

### Secondary Keywords

{Secondary Keywords}

---

## Instructions

Create an SEO-optimized blog outline that:

- Aligns with the user's search intent.
- Naturally incorporates the primary and secondary keywords.
- Organizes content using a clear heading hierarchy.
- Covers the topic comprehensively without unnecessary repetition.
- Includes sections that provide practical value to readers.

If the search intent or topic is ambiguous, explicitly state:

> **Assumption made:** _..._

Do not fabricate statistics, search volume, ranking difficulty, or trending information.

---

## Output Format

### 1. SEO Title

Generate an engaging SEO-friendly title that naturally includes the primary keyword.

---

### 2. Meta Description

Write an SEO-friendly meta description within approximately **150–160 characters**.

---

### 3. Suggested URL Slug

Generate a clean, descriptive, SEO-friendly URL slug.

---

### 4. Search Intent

Identify the most appropriate search intent:

- Informational
- Navigational
- Commercial
- Transactional

Explain your reasoning briefly.

---

### 5. Blog Outline

Provide a structured outline including:

- H1
- H2 headings
- H3 subheadings where appropriate

Ensure the structure follows a logical flow.

---

### 6. Frequently Asked Questions

Generate 5 SEO-relevant FAQ questions that users may search for.

---

### 7. Internal Linking Opportunities

Suggest relevant pages that could be linked internally.

---

### 8. External Reference Suggestions

Recommend authoritative sources or types of sources that would strengthen the article.

Do not invent URLs or citations.

---

## Constraints

- Maintain a professional and informative tone.
- Prioritize readability alongside SEO optimization.
- Avoid keyword stuffing.
- Do not fabricate SEO metrics or statistics.
- Keep recommendations practical and actionable.
- Clearly distinguish assumptions from factual information.
