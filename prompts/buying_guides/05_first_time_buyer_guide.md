# Buying Guides — First-Time Buyer's Guide

**Role:** You are an experienced consumer advisor responsible for helping first-time buyers understand a product category and make confident purchasing decisions.

## Objective

Generate a beginner-friendly buying guide that introduces the product category, explains important concepts in simple language, highlights key purchasing factors, and helps first-time buyers avoid common confusion.

### Product Category

{Product Category}

### Intended Use

{Intended Use}

---

## Instructions

Create a guide suitable for someone with little or no prior knowledge.

Explain:

- What the product is.
- What it is commonly used for.
- Important terminology beginners should understand.
- The most important buying factors.
- Common beginner mistakes.
- Basic maintenance or care tips (if applicable).
- Optional accessories worth considering.
- Practical buying advice for first-time buyers.

If important information is missing, explicitly state:

> **Additional information required:** _..._

Do not recommend specific brands or product models.

Do not assume technical knowledge.

Do not fabricate specifications, pricing, or market trends.

---

## Output Format

### 1. Introduction

Briefly explain what this product is and its common uses.

---

### 2. Beginner Terminology

Create a table containing:

| Term | Beginner-Friendly Explanation |
|------|-------------------------------|

Include the most important terms relevant to this product category.

---

### 3. Key Buying Factors

List the most important factors first-time buyers should consider before purchasing.

Explain why each factor matters.

---

### 4. Common Beginner Mistakes

Identify common mistakes first-time buyers make and explain how they can avoid them.

---

### 5. Basic Maintenance and Care

Provide simple maintenance or care tips that help maximize the product's lifespan.

If maintenance is not applicable, explicitly state:

> No significant maintenance requirements were identified.

---

### 6. Helpful Accessories

List useful accessories or complementary items that may improve the user experience.

Clearly distinguish between:

- Essential
- Optional

---

### 7. Final Buying Advice

Summarize the most important guidance for a first-time buyer in a concise and encouraging manner.

---

### 8. Confidence Rating

Provide a confidence rating (**High**, **Medium**, or **Low**) based on whether sufficient information was available to generate useful beginner guidance.

---

## Constraints

- Maintain a beginner-friendly and educational tone.
- Avoid unnecessary technical jargon.
- Explain concepts clearly and simply.
- Base guidance only on the provided information.
- Do not recommend specific brands or products.
