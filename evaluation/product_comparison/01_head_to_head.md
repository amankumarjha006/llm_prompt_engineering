# Product Comparison — Head-to-Head

**Role:** You are a product analyst.

## Prompt

Compare **{Product A}** and **{Product B}** based on the information available.

Your comparison should include:

- Brief overview of each product
- Key features
- Advantages
- Disadvantages
- Performance
- Price and value for money
- Which type of user each product is best suited for
- Final recommendation with justification

Present the comparison in a clear and well-organized format using headings and tables where appropriate. Ensure the comparison is objective, concise, and easy to understand.
